Common Questions
FREQUENTLY ASKED
How much does window tinting cost in Sydney?
Window tinting costs from $110/sqm for UV protection, $130/sqm for privacy film, $150/sqm for frosted film, and $180/sqm for ceramic film. A typical Sydney home costs between $800–$2,500 depending on windows and film type. Contact us for a free quote.
Can you see through privacy window film?
Yes — privacy film lets you see out clearly during the day while preventing people outside from seeing in. At night when interior lights are on, visibility reverses. For 24/7 privacy, frosted film is the better option.
Does window tinting work in winter?
Absolutely! Window tinting works year round. In winter it helps retain warmth inside your home, reduces cold drafts through glass, and cuts heating bills. It also blocks low winter sun glare.
Will window tinting change how my home looks?
Quality window film is barely noticeable from outside. It won't change the beauty of your home — it will only make it better. Our films have a clean, subtle appearance that complements any style.
Do you service all of Sydney?
Yes! Tintly services all of Sydney including Western Sydney, Parramatta, Hills District, Blacktown, Liverpool, Camden and more. Call 0405 641 320 for a free quote in your area.
How long does installation take?
Most residential jobs are completed in a few hours. We work cleanly and efficiently with minimal disruption to your home or office. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.
